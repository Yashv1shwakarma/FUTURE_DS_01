Business Sales Performance Analytics 📊 Overview

This project looks at sales data from online stores to find out what products are selling well, where people are buying them, and how much money is being made. It helps companies understand what customers are doing and make decisions based on that.

Objectives Look at how sales are going Find out what products are selling the most Discover where the most money is being made Keep track of sales each month and during different seasons Come up with ideas to help businesses do better Dataset

We used a dataset from a store.

This dataset has:

What each product is How many were sold How much they cost When they were sold Where the customers are from Tools & Technologies

We used Microsoft Excel.

We did many things with Excel:

Cleaned up the data Made Pivot Tables Looked at the data Made a dashboard Dashboard Features We looked at how much money was made in total We made graphs to show sales trends We found out what products were selling the most We compared how much money was made in different countries We kept track of how sales were going each month Key Insights The United Kingdom made the most money. Some products were always selling well, making a lot of money. Sales went up during holidays and other busy times. Places like France and Germany also made a lot of money. Business Recommendations We think companies should try to keep customers in the UK happy so they keep buying. They should make sure to have enough of the products that are selling well. They should try to sell more in countries that are growing. They should use what we learned to plan future sales and promotions better. Skills Gained
